# Colors

A color palette, in the digital world, refers to the full range of colors that can be displayed on a device screen or other interface, orin some cases, a collection of colors and tools for use in paint and illustration programs. The color palette reveals a lot about the electronic design of the device or technology, and its visual capabilities for human users.
